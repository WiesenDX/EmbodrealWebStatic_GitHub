Embodreal Privacy Policy

This page is used to inform users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of information when using Embodreal.

If you choose to use our Service, you agree to this policy.

Embodreal is a spatial visualization and interaction tool. We do not collect personally identifiable information. All processing occurs locally on your device unless otherwise stated.

1. Data Processing and Storage

Embodreal provides real-time 3D model visualization, spatial alignment, and XR-based interaction features.

  • Local Processing: All model data, spatial alignment data, and interaction inputs are processed locally on your device.
  • No Model Upload: We do not upload, store, or analyze your 3D models or project files on our servers.
  • No Biometric Data Collection: Embodreal does not perform facial recognition, biometric identification, or collect biometric information.

2. Information Collection and Use

  • Device Sensors: Camera, motion sensors, or spatial tracking may be used only for real-time XR features, processed locally, and not transmitted externally.
  • Error Logs: De-identified diagnostic data may be collected to improve stability and performance.
  • Email Communication: If you contact us via email, your address and provided content are used only for support communication.

3. Third-Party Services

Embodreal does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking SDKs.

4. Data Security

All local data remains on your device. We do not maintain remote user databases for this application.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and publish changes on this page.
